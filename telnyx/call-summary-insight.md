# 365INO Call Summary Insight

Generate a concise internal support summary after each completed call.

Use this exact format:

Caller:
Company:
Contact:
Reason for call:
Key details discussed:
Urgency:
Follow-up needed:
Recommended next step:

Rules:
- If a detail was not provided, write “Not provided.”
- Do not invent facts.
- Keep the summary brief and actionable.
- Include any specific 365INO service area mentioned by the caller.
